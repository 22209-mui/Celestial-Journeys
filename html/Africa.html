MAURITIUS

East Africa

Mauritius is a safe, multicultural island nation in the Indian Ocean, 2,000 km off the African coast, often called a paradise. Known for its year-round tropical climate, it offers luxury resorts, white-sand beaches, and turquoise waters. Beyond the beach, visitors can explore the capital city of Port Louis, hike the Black River Gorges National Park, and see the seven-coloured earth of Chamarel. Its unique culture blends African, Indian, Chinese, and European influences, resulting in vibrant, diverse food and festivals.
