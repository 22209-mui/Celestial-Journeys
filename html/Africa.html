AFRICA

EAST AFRICA

MAURITIUS

A safe, multicultural island nation in the Indian Ocean, 2000 km off the African coast. Known for its year-round tropical climate, it offers luxury resorts, white-sand beaches and turquoise waters. Beyond the beach, visitors can explore Port Louis, hike the Black River Gorges National Park, and see the seven-coloured earth of Chamarel. Its culture blends African, Indian, Chinese, and European influences resulting in vibrant food and festivals.

SOUTH AFRICA

TABLE MOUNTAIN

One of the world's most iconic natural landmarks, Table Mountain overlooks Cape Town with its famously flat summit. Ride the rotating cable car to the top for panoramic views stretching across the Atlantic Ocean and the Cape Peninsula. The mountain is home to an extraordinary range of flora found nowhere else on Earth, and its surrounds offer world-class hiking, vibrant neighbourhoods, and celebrated cuisine.
