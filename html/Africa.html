MAURITIUS

East Africa

Mauritius is a breathtaking East African island nation set in the turquoise waters of the Indian Ocean, often hailed as a slice of paradise where dream holidays become reality. Beyond its stunning white-sand beaches, this vibrant, multicultural destination offers lush rainforests, volcanic mountains, and a rich blend of culinary delights. Perfectly positioned for luxury resorts, adventure activities, and romantic getaways, it’s a year-round destination that mixes high-end tourism with an authentic, welcoming local culture. Located roughly 2,000 km off the southeast coast of the African continent, Mauritius offers more than just the sun, sea, and sand experience. Visitors can explore the bustling, historic capital of Port Louis, hike through the rugged Black River Gorges National Park, or witness the geological wonder of the seven-coloured earth in Chamarel. As a former trading port, it boasts a unique blend of African, Indian, Chinese, and European influences, evident in its vibrant markets, varied festivals, and fusion food, making it an incredibly diverse and safe destination to explore.
