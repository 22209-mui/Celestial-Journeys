EUROPE

FRANCE

PARIS

The City of Light needs no introduction. Paris enchants with its grand boulevards, world-class museums including the Louvre and Musée d'Orsay, and the glittering iron silhouette of the Eiffel Tower. Wander Montmartre's cobbled streets, savour a croissant at a pavement café, and lose yourself in the elegance of the Marais. From haute couture to hidden bistros, Paris sets the standard for beauty, culture, and romance.

ENGLAND

LONDON

One of the world's great capital cities, London is a living tapestry of history and modernity. Marvel at Buckingham Palace and the Tower of London, then cross the Thames to the Tate Modern and Borough Market. World-class theatre lines the West End, while diverse neighbourhoods from Notting Hill to Brick Lane offer endless exploration. A city where centuries of heritage sit comfortably alongside cutting-edge art, cuisine, and culture.
