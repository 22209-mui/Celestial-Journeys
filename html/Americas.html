THE AMERICAS

USA

LAS VEGAS

Rising improbably from the Mojave Desert, Las Vegas is the world's ultimate entertainment capital. The famed Strip dazzles with mega-resort casinos, Cirque du Soleil (famous circus) spectaculars, and world-class restaurants helmed by celebrity chefs. Beyond the neon, explore the raw grandeur of Red Rock Canyon and the Hoover Dam. Whether you come for the shows, the tables, the pools, or the sheer spectacle, Las Vegas never fails to astonish.

PERU

MACHU PICCHU

Perched at 2,430 metres in the Peruvian Andes, Machu Picchu is one of humanity's greatest achievements. This 15th-century Inca citadel emerges from swirling cloud and jungle, its stone terraces and temples precisely aligned with the cosmos. Trek the legendary Inca Trail or arrive by scenic train through the Sacred Valley. The surrounding region of Cusco offers further Inca heritage, vibrant markets, and Andean cuisine unlike anything else on Earth.
