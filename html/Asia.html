ASIA

JAPAN

TOKYO

A city of thrilling contradictions, Tokyo pulses with neon-lit energy while ancient shrines and tea houses offer moments of profound stillness. Explore the controlled chaos of Shibuya Crossing, lose yourself in the retro arcades of Akihabara, and find peace at Senso-ji Temple in Asakusa. World-renowned for its extraordinary food culture, from Michelin-starred dining to flawless street ramen. Tokyo is an unmatched sensory experience.

SOUTHEAST ASIA

SINGAPORE

A gleaming city-state where futuristic architecture meets lush tropical greenery, Singapore is one of Asia's most remarkable destinations. Stroll through the iconic Gardens by the Bay, shop along Orchard Road, and explore the vivid heritage districts of Chinatown, Little India, and Kampong Glam. Singapore's food scene is legendary. From hawker centres serving laksa and char kway teow to some of the finest restaurants in the world.
