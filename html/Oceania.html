SOUTH PACIFIC OCEAN

FIJI

An archipelago of over 300 islands scattered across the South Pacific, Fiji is synonymous with paradise. Crystal-clear lagoons, vibrant coral reefs, and powder-soft beaches make it one of the world's premier dive and snorkel destinations. Inland, explore lush highland villages, cascading waterfalls, and the warm hospitality of Fijian culture, where the legendary greeting of "Bula!" sets the tone for every encounter.

NEW ZEALAND

QUEENSTOWN

Located on the shores of Lake Wakatipu and framed by the jagged Remarkables mountain range, Queenstown is New Zealand's ultimate adventure capital. Bungee jumping, skydiving, jet boating, and world-class skiing are all on offer amid spectacular alpine scenery. Beyond the adrenaline, the town boasts a thriving food and wine culture, with Central Otago pinot noir among the finest in the Southern Hemisphere.
